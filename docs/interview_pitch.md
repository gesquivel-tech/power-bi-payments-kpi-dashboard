# Interview Pitch

This is a small Power BI portfolio project based on synthetic payment data.

The goal was to practice how to turn raw operational data into business-friendly KPIs and dashboards. I focused on data modeling, DAX measures, validation logic, and dashboard design for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

The project includes common reporting metrics such as total payments, successful payments, failed payments, failure rate, processed amount, SLA breach rate, and errors by type.

I also included SQL validation queries to show how I would compare Power BI measures against the source data before trusting a KPI in a business report.

This kind of workflow is aligned with reporting and business systems roles where accuracy, documentation, stakeholder communication, and data quality are as important as the dashboard visuals.
